Common Questions

CONSTRUCTION
QUERIES

What types of construction projects does Myaluza Civils handle?
Myaluza Civils specialises in two core areas: Civil Engineering — including water reticulation, concrete structures (chambers, retaining walls, culverts), and roads (surfacing, sidewalks, paving, kerbing and channelling) — and General Building, encompassing new builds, renovations, and structural extensions.
Is Myaluza Civils BBBEE compliant for government tenders?
Yes. Myaluza Civils holds BBBEE Level 1 status and is 100% Black-Owned, making them an ideal partner for both government and private sector projects seeking to meet transformation procurement requirements. All compliance documentation is available on request.
What is Myaluza Civils' CIDB grading?
Myaluza Civils is registered with the Construction Industry Development Board (CIDB) at grade 5 GB PE (General Building) and grade 6 CE PE (Civil Engineering). These gradings reflect their capacity to undertake substantial government and private infrastructure contracts.
Where does Myaluza Civils operate?
The company is headquartered in Pietermaritzburg (102 Trelawney Road, Fairmade) with branch offices in Greytown (Emabovini) and Durban (Glenwood). They service projects across KwaZulu-Natal and the wider South African region.
How can I request a joint venture or partnership?
Use the "Request Our Intelligence" form on this page to select "Joint Venture / Partnership" as your reason, or contact us directly at admin@myaluzacivils.co.za or call 078 349 2494. Our team will respond within one business day with our Company Profile and relevant credentials.
What makes Myaluza Civils different from other construction contractors?
Founded by hands-on practitioners with deep field experience, Myaluza Civils blends traditional construction values with innovative methods — reflected in our tagline "Where Tradition Meets Innovation." As a 100% Black-Owned, BBBEE Level 1 entity, we also directly support South Africa's transformation objectives while delivering technical excellence.
